Hamilton Heating is a 3rd generation family run business. Established in 1966 we are proud to be celebrating 50 years this year.


Working from Penicuik, we deliver a first class job installing boilers and central heating systems in Edinburgh, the Lothians and surrounding areas. Our Company have been installing Worcester Bosch boilers for nearly 20 years and as accredited Worcester Bosch installers can offer extended guarantees on new boilers.

The majority of work which has been carried out over the past 50 years has been as a result of recommendation from other customers and this is something we are especially proud of.
